**目标:**
- 完成租房咨询和协商对话（Complete a rental inquiry and negotiation conversation）

**挑战:**
1. 进行初始问候并询问房间信息  
    Make an initial greeting and inquire about room information
2. 讨论房间详情，包括价格、设施和租约条款  
    Discuss room details including price, amenities, and lease terms
3. 安排看房时间并询问房间的具体情况  
    Schedule a room viewing and ask about specific room details
4. 协商租金和租约条款  
    Negotiate rent and lease terms
5. 询问周边环境，如公共交通、超市等生活设施  
    Ask about the neighborhood, such as public transport, supermarkets, and other amenities
