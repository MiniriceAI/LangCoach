- Difficulty Level: {difficulty}

{conversation_transcript}

**Task:** Evaluate the user's English learning performance based on these criteria. Be realistic, fair, and encouraging. Provide honest scores that reflect actual performance.

**Scoring Guidelines:**
- 90-100: Excellent (优秀) - Near-native proficiency for this level
- 70-89: Good (良好) - Competent with minor issues
- 50-69: Fair (及格) - Basic competence with notable gaps
- 0-49: Needs improvement (需改进) - Significant difficulties

**IMPORTANT:** 
- Provide realistic scores based on actual performance
- Avoid giving all the same scores - differentiate between dimensions
- Consider the stated difficulty level when scoring
- Be specific in feedback, mention concrete examples when possible
- Output MUST be valid JSON only, no extra text before or after

**Evaluation Dimensions:**

1. **Grammar Accuracy (grammar_score: 0-100):** Evaluate correctness of verb tenses, subject-verb agreement, articles, prepositions, sentence structure. Look for errors and patterns.

2. **Vocabulary Usage (vocabulary_score: 0-100):** Assess appropriateness of vocabulary for the scenario, diversity (not repetitive), and sophistication level matching the difficulty.

3. **Fluency & Coherence (fluency_score: 0-100):** Evaluate natural flow of responses, logical coherence, ability to maintain conversation. Note: shorter responses or hesitation patterns may indicate lower fluency.

4. **Task Completion (task_completion_score: 0-100):** How well did the user achieve the conversation goal? Did they address the scenario requirements? Did they provide relevant information?

5. **Overall Performance (overall_score: 0-100):** Holistic assessment combining all dimensions. Should be close to the average of the four scores above.
