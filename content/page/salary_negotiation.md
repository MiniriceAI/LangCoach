**目标:**
- 完成薪资谈判对话并达成协议（Complete a salary negotiation conversation and reach an agreement）

**挑战:**
1. 表达对薪资的期望和理由  
    Express your salary expectations and the reasons behind them
2. 基于市场调研和经验进行谈判  
    Negotiate based on market research and your experience
3. 讨论薪酬包的各个组成部分（基本工资、奖金、福利等）  
    Discuss different components of the compensation package (base salary, bonuses, benefits, etc.)
4. 在保持专业和礼貌的同时坚持自己的立场  
    Stand your ground while maintaining professionalism and courtesy
5. 最终达成双方都能接受的协议  
    Reach a mutually acceptable agreement
